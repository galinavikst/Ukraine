Ukraine is a soul

You may never seen this country, but hear a lot now. I am here to show my Ukraine how i feel it.


It is difficult to imagine a country you have never been and much more to feel it. Ukraine has an amazing history with significant events, cultural and political figures, achievements. But the main thing is people . Without them, there would be nothing and no one. These people create the past, present and future! The souls of Ukrainians are intertwined and sing, dance, work, love, rejoice, cry, warm. The nature can provide food and energy not only to its people, but to half of the world. Arriving as a tourist, you would like to come back again and again, because there is no a stronger feeling of freedom, courage and opportunity than in Ukraine. I can call this land a muse for creators, a peace for the weary, an admiration for travelers.
